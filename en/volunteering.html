Volunteering

Mozilla

On my free time, I invest a significant time for the Mozilla foundation and her mission. I go on events to hold the stand and talk about what Mozilla do and her mission. I also attend to community events like meetups, hackathon, MozFest or WednesdayDoc (Localize the MDN documentation). I also love to write code in order to extend Mozilla projects like Firefox OS app, browser webextension or aframe component.

mozilla.com

Open Food Facts

During a hackathon in Mozilla Paris office, I helped to develop the Firefox OS app of Open Food Facts. Open Food Facts is a collaborative project which tries to list all the food products from all around the world.

ca.openfoodfacts.org
